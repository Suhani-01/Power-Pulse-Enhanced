Power Distribution in Delhi

Electricity distribution in Delhi is managed by three major private DISCOMs and two government/defense agencies. Together, they ensure uninterrupted supply across the entire city.

Major Distribution Companies (DISCOMs)

  • BSES Rajdhani Power Limited (BRPL)

    • Covers South and West Delhi.
    • Serves more than 2.5 million consumers.
    • Joint venture of Reliance Infrastructure and Government of NCT Delhi.

  • BSES Yamuna Power Limited (BYPL)

    • Covers Central and East Delhi.
    • Serves around 1.7 million consumers.
    • Also a joint venture of Reliance Infrastructure and GNCTD.

  • Tata Power Delhi Distribution Limited (TPDDL)

    • Covers North and North-West Delhi.
    • Serves over 1.8 million consumers.
    • Joint venture of Tata Power and Government of NCT Delhi.

Other Agencies

  • New Delhi Municipal Council (NDMC)

    • A municipal body (not a company).
    • Supplies electricity in Lutyens’ Delhi, Connaught Place, diplomatic areas, and nearby VIP zones.

  • Military Engineering Services (MES)

    • A defense organization under the Ministry of Defence.
    • Manages electricity distribution in cantonment and military areas of Delhi.
